Kurt

Kurt is a traditional product that belongs to the Kazakh culture. It is made by drying fermented milk, from which yogurt is also obtained. When it becomes thick, the oldest female, often helped by children, works the fermented milk to make its characteristic round shape, the size of an apricot.
